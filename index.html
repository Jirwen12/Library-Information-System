Hello! I'm Jirwen Palaje, a passionate IT student at PLT College of Guinobatan, Incorporated. I have a deep interest in technology, web development, and creating innovative solutions. With a focus on building user-friendly systems and applications, I aim to improve the digital experience for students and the community. Currently, I am working on various projects, including Student Information Systems, Library Information System and Barangay Census Tools, designed to streamline processes and make life easier. I enjoy combining modern design with functionality, ensuring that my work is not only effective but also visually appealing. As the president of the Katipunang Kabataan of Barangay Mapaco and as the College Student Council President at PLT College, I’m always looking for opportunities to contribute to my community, whether through technology or leadership. I’m constantly learning, and I am excited to continue improving my skills and knowledge to create better solutions. Feel free to explore my work, and I hope it inspires you as much as it inspires me!
